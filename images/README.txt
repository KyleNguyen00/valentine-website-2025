Optional: Add Studio Ghibli images here

1. Go to https://www.ghibli.jp/works/ and open a film (e.g. Totoro, Ponyo).
2. Click "作品静止画" (film stills) and download images you like.
3. Save them in this folder (e.g. totoro.jpg, ponyo.jpg).
4. In config.js, set ghibliImages URLs to local paths, e.g. "images/totoro.jpg".

The site will then use your Ghibli images instead of the placeholder photos.
